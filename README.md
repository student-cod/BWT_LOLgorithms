# BWT_LOLgorithms
TrustLedger turns UPI transaction history into a verified Financial Identity Card for India's 400 million gig workers and informal earners. Upload your transactions, get a Behavioral Trust Score, generate a shareable card, and let any lender verify it instantly — no salary slip needed. Built for Bharat. 🇮🇳

### Financial Identity for the Invisible Workforce

> Turning digital transaction history into a verified Financial Trust Score.

---

## The Problem

Millions of gig workers and informal earners in India receive regular income through UPI and digital platforms.  
But because they don’t have salary slips or traditional credit history, they remain invisible to banks and lenders.

No paperwork = No loans.

---

## The Solution

**TrustLedger** converts UPI transaction history into a clean, shareable Financial Identity.

Users can:
- Upload transaction CSV  
- Get a Behavioral Trust Score (0–100)  
- Generate a shareable Financial Identity Card  
- Let lenders verify it instantly  

No salary slip required.

---

## How It Works

1. Transaction data is parsed and categorized.
2. Income stability and spending behavior are analyzed.
3. A Trust Score is generated using transparent scoring logic.
4. A verified identity card is created with a unique verification ID.
5. Lenders can validate the card through a secure verification portal.
